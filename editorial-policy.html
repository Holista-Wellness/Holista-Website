Editorial Policy

How Holista approaches wellness content.

Holista publishes practical wellness articles focused on sleep rituals, skin-care habits, weight-loss habits, and everyday wellness routines. We aim for a tone that is calm, clear, and useful rather than sensational or overly prescriptive.

Editorial standards

  • Content is written to support general education and everyday decision-making.
  • Articles are reviewed for clarity, accuracy, and consistency before publication.
  • Recommendations are framed as flexible guidance rather than rigid rules.

Story development

Topics are chosen based on common reader questions, everyday wellness concerns, and practical habits that can make healthy routines feel easier to sustain. We favor stories that bring useful structure to a topic without overpromising outcomes.

Updates and revisions

We may revise articles over time to improve accuracy, readability, or relevance. When meaningful changes are made, article pages may reflect an updated date.

Editorial independence

Holista aims to keep editorial decisions separate from advertising or sponsorship considerations. Any future paid partnerships should be clearly labeled.
