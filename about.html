About Holista

Hi, I'm Kate Thompson

Editor. Wellness observer. Person with excellent pillowcase opinions.

I created Holista to explore the small routines, beauty habits, and everyday health ideas people return to when they want life to feel a little calmer and more intentional.

Our approach is practical, curious, and fond of atmosphere. We look for the tiny behavioral cue inside each trend, then focus on what feels realistic enough to fit into an ordinary day.

Every article is written for readers who enjoy a good ritual, prefer realistic expectations, and want wellness writing that feels polished without feeling impossible.
